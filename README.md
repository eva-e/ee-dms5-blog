## Course Title: Digital Media Studio 5: Audiovision > Course Code: COMM 2747

## Assignment 4: Blog >

<p align="left">This assessment takes the format of a weekly blog. It captures the creative exercises you have worked on during Wednesday classes. It also provides an opportunity for you to reflect on the creative process, where appropriate, the lessons you have learned which might be taken forward in your practice, whilst evaluating the works produced during that time - both yours and those of your peers. More specific instructions for the blog related to the particular week's exercises will be provided in class each week. The four main skills we will be focussing on in this studio  - of which the blog provides evidence - relate to creativity, evaluation/analysis, communication and your contribution to a community of practice. You will be undertaking all these activities across the semester and your grade for Ass4 will reflect all four areas.  The blog will also be used for you to show your progress in your major projects for Ass4. Works in progress, screenshots and reflections on the collaboration will all be used.
</p>
